Welcome


Take the stress out of your daily commute! TravelSafeTT is a mobile app that allows you to travel with confidence, knowing that you're safe.


Features


Secure

Enjoy a secure riding experience with verified drivers and real-time tracking as you ride.

Scan

Our scannable QR codes allow you to see who your driver is and their vehicle identification before starting the ride.

Share

Share your live location with your loved ones as you travel, ensuring that they can always know where you are.


Rider



Your Rider Dashboard holds all your latest activity that could be accessed anytime and anywhere! It also allows you to access the QR Code Scanner.


When you scan a QR Code, the driver's picture, number plates and vehicle pictures are presented to you for verification. The driver is ONLY trustworthy if the information presented is what you see.

Disclaimer! If the vehicle's driver is not the same as the one in the picture, DO NOT enter the vehicle and REPORT them immediately!

Driver



When your account has been activated, you will be able to access your Driver Dashboard with your QR Code and Public Information.


Place your QR Code in an easily accessbile and scannable area for passengers!
